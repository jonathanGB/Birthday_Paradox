The Birthday Paradox

This is a famous probability paradox. If you had a class of n people, what would be the probability that none of them shared a same birthday date?
Sometimes, our intuition is just plain wrong, especially in this case!

How to use?

Click on the "Group Size" input, then use your directional keys (↑ & ↓), or the arrows displayed in the input.

You can also type an integer from 2 to 100 and then click "Calculate Table"
